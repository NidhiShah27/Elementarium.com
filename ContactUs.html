Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the drag-and-drop equation builder?
Simply click and drag any element from the periodic table into the equation bar. You can combine multiple elements to create chemical equations and see how they react in real-time!
Is the Interactive Periodic Table free to use?
Yes! Our platform is completely free for students, teachers, and chemistry enthusiasts. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.
How can I track my quiz progress?
The quiz section automatically tracks your score and shows you correct answers with detailed explanations. Complete all three levels to test your mastery of the periodic table!
